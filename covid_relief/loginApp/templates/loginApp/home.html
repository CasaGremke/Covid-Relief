Recomended Procedures for Daily Activities

  • In general, the more closely you interact with others and the longer that interaction, the higher the risk of COVID-19 spread.
  • If you decide to engage in public activities, continue to protect yourself by practicing everyday preventive actions.
  • Keep these items on hand when venturing out: a face mask, tissues, and a hand sanitizer with at least 60% alcohol, if possible.

Best Practices would be to:

  • Wash your hands often. This should be for at least 20 seconds. Wash your hand after interacting with people or touching things that a lot of people touch. Also, when avaliable use sanitizer with at least 60% alcohol. (Note: this does not replace hand washing)
  • Social Distance when possible. The recomended distance is 6ft or further. Try to avoid situations that don't allow social distancing.
  • Wear a mask. Masks should be worn over the nose and mouth. Masks should be worn even when social distancing. A mask will protect others if you are infected and not showing symtoms. It will also give you some protection from the virus.

Recomended Procedures for Travel

  • Travel is not recomended if you or anyone you live with would be vulnerable to COVID-19.
  • Make sure to check travel restrictions before you go.
  • Follow the daily recomendations on your trip.
  • Consider your form of transport. Public transport or air travel made bring you into contact with a lot of people. Traveling by car or RV will still expose you at stops for gas or food.
  • It is better to bring what you can so you don't have to stop at a lot of places.
  • After you return you should be extra careful incase you have contracted COVID-19.
